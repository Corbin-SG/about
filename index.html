ABOUT ME

I want to write books, I want to create art for my stories, and I want to develop games around them. My skills are all very basic at the moment but each day I strive to improve them.

Outside of previously mentioned interests, I also enjoy music, shows, and a few games. I typically spend my free time playing games or watching shows. I've also been taking some time out of each day to learn other languages (mainly Dutch). I read a lot as a child but have struggled to make time for it as of late.

I grew up in Ontario and have not travelled much. The one big trip I took as a child was to Jamaica for a week. I hope to travel more in the future. I'm the eldest child with two younger brothers. We were moved schools a lot as a child (I went to three schools in one year once) but by middle school we were both in a consistent location. I tried many ways of using my creativity as a child but didn't stick with anything until I was in high school and finally managed to finish a piece of writing.
